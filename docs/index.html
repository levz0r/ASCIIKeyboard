Features

Everything you need to express yourself in ASCII

[=]

Menu Bar Native

Lives quietly in your menu bar. Toggle on/off with a click. No windows cluttering your workspace.

{Aa}

20 Unique Fonts

From Banner to Doom, Standard to Star Wars. Pick the perfect style for every message.

[>>]

Live Preview

See your ASCII art as you type in a floating preview window. What you see is what you get.

|_|

Works Everywhere

Type in any app - Slack, Discord, Terminal, Notes. ASCII Keyboard outputs directly where you're typing.

{#}

Keyboard Shortcuts

ESC to cancel, Backspace to edit. Your regular shortcuts (Cmd+C, etc.) work normally.

[*]

Launch at Login

Start automatically when you boot up. Always ready when inspiration strikes.

Three simple steps to ASCII glory

1

Click the Menu Bar Icon

Toggle ASCII Keyboard ON from your menu bar

Press Enter
