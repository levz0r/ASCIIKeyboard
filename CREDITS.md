## FIGlet

FIGlet (Frank, Ian & Glenn's Letters) is a program for making large letters out of ordinary text. While this application implements its own FIGlet font parser, we acknowledge the FIGlet project and its contributors.

- FIGlet Website: http://www.figlet.org/
- Original Authors: Glenn Chappell, Ian Chai, and Frank
